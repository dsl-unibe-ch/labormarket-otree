Welcome!

You are about to participate in a study on decision-making in an organizational setting. Please read the instructions carefully. You need to understand these instructions to make money today. In total, this session should last no longer than 2 hours.

We promise to carry out this study in the manner described in the instructions, with no deception of any form. If you have any questions at any time during today's session, please raise your hand. The administrator will answer your question in private.

You will be paid a {{ participation_fee }} show-up fee plus any amount you earn during today's session. You can earn additional income, which will be explained in more detail later. You will be paid in cash at the end of today's session.

Overview

In this study, some of you will act as workers and some of you will act as employers.

If you are an employer, you will be responsible for hiring a worker to work for you. If you are a worker, you will decide which employer to work for and how much effort to exert for that employer.

You are assigned to the role of {{ if player.role == "Employee" }} a Worker. {{ else }} an Employer. {{ endif }} You will keep this role throughout the entire study. All other participants in this session will also keep their roles throughout the entire study.

The decisions you and the other participants make will determine your compensation, which will be first provided in points. At the end of today's session, earned points will be converted into cash at the rate of $0.25 for every 100 points earned.

Your participation will be completed entirely on the computer, and your roles will be completely anonymous. That is, you will never know the role of any other person in this room, and no one will know your role. You will only be identified by a randomly assigned color "alias" in the experiment. For example, in the experiment you might be known as "aquamarine."

This study consists of 10 periods. After finishing all periods, you will complete a questionnaire about your experience in the study. Then, you will be asked to provide demographic information. Your responses to all these questions will be anonymous.

Study Instructions

On the following screens, you will learn about the study instructions. After each section of instructions, you will be required to pass a comprehension quiz on the information you just learned. These quizzes are designed to ensure that you have paid attention to and understood the instructions. There are three comprehension quizzes. You must correctly answer all of the quiz questions prior to continuing with the study.
