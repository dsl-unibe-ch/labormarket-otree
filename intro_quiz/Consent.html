You will be paid a {{ participation_fee }} show-up fee for participating in this study. Depending on the choices you make, you can receive an additional payment of up to $20. The study is expected to last between 75 and 90 minutes.

There are no anticipated risks associated with your participation in this study and you may withdraw from the study at any time. However, you must complete the study in order to receive compensation.

If you have any questions or concerns regarding this study, you may contact Ivo Tafkov at itafkov@gsu.edu.
