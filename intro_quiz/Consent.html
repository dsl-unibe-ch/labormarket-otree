Title: Labor Market Study
Principal Investigator: Ivo Tafkov (Georgia State University)

Procedures

You are being asked to participate in a research study. If you decide to participate, you will stay in this room and continue with the study, which will be completed during this session. You will be provided with detailed instructions about your task. During the experiment, you will be asked to provide demographic information. At the end of the experiment, you will complete a questionnaire, which will ask you questions about how you made your decisions. Your responses to these questions will be anonymous. Participation in the study will require no longer than 2 hours of your time.

Compensation

You will be paid $5.00 as a show-up fee plus you have the opportunity to receive additional compensation. However, the actual payment will vary depending on your performance.

Voluntary Participation and Withdrawal

Participation in research is voluntary. You do not have to be in this study. If you decide to be in the study and change your mind, you have the right to drop out at any time. You may skip questions or stop participating at any time. If you do drop out, you will not receive any additional compensation from the study.

Contact Information

Contact Ivo Tafkov at 404-413-7226 and itafkov@gsu.edu if you have questions, concerns, or complaints about this study.
