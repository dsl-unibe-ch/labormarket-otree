Employers begin each period with an initial endowment of {{ manager_endowment }} points.

Employer does not hire any worker

If an employer does not hire any worker in a period, he/she will only receive the endowment in this period. So, if the employer does not offer any contracts or no worker accepts any of the employer's contract offers, the employer's earnings for this period are: 

    Employer earnings for the period

    = {{ manager_endowment }} (initial endowment)

Employer hires and does not offer training

If a worker accepts a contract offer of an employer that does not include training, the employer receives the following revenues and incurs the following costs:

  • Total revenue based on the hired worker's productivity (determined by his/her skill level) and the effort level the worker chooses in this period. Specifically,
    Employer revenue = worker effort level × worker productivity
  • Total costs based on the amount of salary paid to the hired worker.

So, if a worker accepts a contract that does not include training, the employer's earnings for this period are:

    Employer earnings for the period

    = {{ manager_endowment }} (initial endowment)

    + worker effort level x worker productivity (employer revenue)

    – salary paid to worker

Employer hires and does offer training

If a worker accepts a contract offer of an employer that does include training, the employer receives the following revenues and incurs the following costs:

  • Total revenue equals half of the revenue based on the hired worker's productivity (determined by his/her skill level) and the effort level the worker chooses in this period. Specifically,
    Employer revenue = 0.5 × worker effort level × worker productivity
  • Total costs equal the direct cost of 50 points for the training and the salary paid to the hired worker.

So, if a worker accepts a contract that does include training, the employer's earnings for this period are: 

    Employer earnings for the period
    = {{ manager_endowment }} (initial endowment)
    + 0.5 × [worker effort level × worker productivity] (employer revenue)
    – 50 (direct training costs)
    – salary paid to worker

Payout of Earnings

Your cumulative earnings for all 10 periods of the study will be converted to cash (at the rate of $0.25 per 100 points). This will be added to your {{ participation_fee }} participation fee. The resulting amount will be paid to you in cash at the end of today’s session.
